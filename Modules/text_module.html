Code of Conduct

We have set these code of conduct guidelines to ensure each resident feels protected and equal in the community

1. Do not eat other community members.

2. Personal space must be maintained at all times.

3. No derogatory language is permitted.

Failure to comply with these guidelines will result in one strike with a maximum of three strikes.

After three strikes, the resident will be suspended from the premises for a period of time that will be discussed by the Code of Conduct Committee.
